Our Pricing Structure

How we price web design and development projects

This is usually the first question new clients ask? This is not an easy question to answer without a deep understanding of what the client requires. The main component in the price is the cost of labour of the developer(s) but there are additional expenses which will dictate the final cost of the project.

Website Structure

The first element to analyse is the size of the website in terms of number of pages. Does the client require a single page scrolling webiste or a website with several or tens of pages layed out in a hierarchical nature which may be several layers deep.

Content

Once the structure of the website is confirmed, content needs to created, aggregated and approved. A client who has already prepared content and indeed knows where the content will be placed within the website structure will obviously not need the services of a content writer and therefore will avoid the associated cost. As you can appreciate time taken in the creation and approval of content can lead to project creep. Project creep can lead to expense which will inflate overal costs.

Images and Other Media

In an ideal world everyone would have access to their own high resolution images, video and any other media which would be of use on their new website. This is rarely the case, therefore it is often neccessary to leverage other options to fulfill the requiremnt. It is possible to hire a photographer, a videographer etc. or design custom graphics or buy stock media which is readily available. It is important to get the mix between stock and custom images and other media right while staying within the project budget constraints.

Project Timeframe

If the client requires the project to "Go Live" in a very short time frame, development time for a project of a certain size will be more or less be fixed therfore long working hours for developers will obviously inflate costs. These urgent projects will often work flawlessly if the client is well prepared in terms of prior aggregation of content and pre-determined website structure.

Project Quotation

Having reviewed all the above, we can in consultation with the client look at possible solutions that may reduce costs. We will always work with the client to provide an accurate approximation of an achieveable project timeline. Ultimately we will produce a quotation for the project that reflects all of the aforemnetioned.
