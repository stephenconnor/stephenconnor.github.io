ConDev - About Us

Who am I?

My name Stephen Connor the owner and founder of ConDev. ConDev, what's in a name? ConDev is an abbreviation of Connor Software Development, a new Web Development and Design startup based in Dublin.

About Me

Having worked in International Freight Forwarding with leading Irish owned and multi-national companies for several years, I decided to reskill choosing computer programming as my new field of study and future career. I successfully completed my BSc in Digital Technology and Design, subsequently gaining experience within software development teams. I soon realised than many companies have an internet presence which isn’t reaching its full potential, whether that be a website that is not responsive / mobile device friendly or their website is not promoting the company’s offerings to the utmost or the website’s user experience is not what it should be. In founding ConDev, I aim to help other business's maximise their internet presence and thus drive new business development.

Having built very strong skills in management roles and a deep knowledge of customer relationship management, I know how important it is to guide clients through each and every process. During the website design and development process ConDev will keep each and every stage as simple as possible.

Website CMS Training Packages

CMS Training

The Content Management System (CMS) training package allows customers to learn how to use, maintain and update their own website. Following completion of training, a client will be able to maintain and update their website, thus eliminating most of the costs involved in these processes. Each training package will be customised to suit the client's requirements. Topics covered include: log-in/log-out; profile management; publishing and editing blog posts; creating and editing pages; adding images and other forms of media; Website backup and restore; Installing and Updating Plugins.
